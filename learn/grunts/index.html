Grunts

Grunts are just composable classes that didn't fit in anywhere else.

  • text-trim: Set line-height to 1
  • text-center: Sets text-align to center
  • text-left: Sets text-align to left
  • text-right: Sets text-align to right
  • grid-gap-margin: Sets grid-gap to margin
  • grid-gap-border-width: Sets grid-gap to border-width
  • grid-gap-text-padding: Sets grid-gap to text-padding
  • grid-gap-text-margin: Sets grid-gap to text-margin
  • flatten: removes shadows of self and children
  • no-borders: removes borders of self and children
  • no-margins: removes margins of self and children
  • shade: Makes the text black
  • tint: Makes the text white
  • trim: Removes bottom margin
  • trim-inner: Removes bottom margin of inner elements
  • trim-all: Removes bottom margin of self and children
  • hidden: hidden on all breakpoints
  • clearfix: clears floats

Bump

The bump composable moves things around by 2px. Combine it with up, down, left and right to specify which direction.

CSS properties

Every CSS property has a default Less variable in Cavepaint. Many of them also have their own classes, or can be set up easily with their own classes with the default() mixin.
