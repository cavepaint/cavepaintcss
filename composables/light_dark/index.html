Details

  • class: light, dark
  • settings: fill-opacity, pattern-base, output-lighter
  • defaults: none
composable works with colors

Examples

The .light composable makes an element lighter. It works well with color classes, for easy-to-use class pairings like "light graphite":

The only acceptable form of wrestling is gator wrestling.

<p class="graphite padding">The only acceptable form of wrestling is gator wrestling.</p>

With the .light class, the paragraph becomes lighter.

The only acceptable form of wrestling is gator wrestling.

<p class="light graphite padding">The only acceptable form of wrestling is gator wrestling.</p>

This composable sets the background-image property, so it may have problems with elements that use a background image.
