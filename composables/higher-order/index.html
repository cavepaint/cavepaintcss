Card

Basic cards

Cards are a set of defaults for making content look like a card on the page:

Welcome to the club.

Don't forget to validate parking!

<div class="card">
    <h3>Welcome to the club.</h3>
    <p>Don't forget to validate parking!</p>
    </div>

Welcome to the club.

Don't forget to validate parking!

<div class="card">
    <h3 class="info padding margin-bottom">Welcome to the club.</h3>
    <p>Don't forget to validate parking!</p>
    </div>

Cards have an undecorated default look, but classes like inset, dark and no-border can rectify that:

Welcome to the club.

    <h3>Welcome to the club.</h3>
    <p>Don't forget to validate parking!</p>
    </div>
<div class="card no-border">
    <h3>Welcome to the club.</h3>

    </div>
